When Seu talks about defamiliarizing our tools, I believe she is talking about taking digital tools and presenting them in unconventional manners that make us think about their intentions and purpose.  I think this action relates to poetry because you can communicate and express ideas when you defamiliarize digital tools.

The category “Removing Function from Form” involves taking away the intended function of elements found on a website or software (e.g. a button, scrollbar) and experimenting with them to find other interesting ways these elements can function.

  ASCII Cam

I feel that the site ASCII Cam, works within this category because the site uses ASCII in a way that deviates from it's original function and gives it a different function, in this case, responding to what is on your webcam.
