Websites as Cooking Homemade Food

  • Like homemade food, websites are made using different “ingredients”.
  • These ingredients being prepared with different tools similar to kitchenware, ovenware, and etc.
  • You could use the languages CSS or Javascript to make your website background blue.
  • Similar to how you can choose to use a knife and/or a processor to chop food.

Maybe you're just starting out - having little to no experience in making a website, you turn to "recipes", guidelines, and other people in how to make it. Or maybe you are a more experienced “cook” who knows recipes and rules by heart, which gives you limitless possibilites to experiment with how certain ingredients are prepared, cooked, and presented.
